Description

This brownie recipe makes rich, fudgy brownies that always turn out well. They're topped with homemade chocolate frosting to make the best brownies ever!

Ingredients Needed:

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder

How to Make Brownies Step-By-Step

  1. Melt the butter on the stove.
  2. Remove butter from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.
  3. Beat in the dry ingredients.
  4. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
